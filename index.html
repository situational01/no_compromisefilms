Behind the Lens

I am a storyteller who refuses to compromise.

With over twelve years behind the camera, I've built No Compromise Films on a single belief: every frame must tell the truth. As a documentary photographer, cinematographer, producer, and creative director, I bring a holistic vision to every project — from conception to final cut.

My work spans the raw energy of sports arenas, the untamed beauty of wildlife in its natural habitat, and the intimate moments of everyday life across cultures. I don't stage moments. I wait for them. I earn them.

Based between Nairobi and London, I collaborate with brands, publications, and NGOs that value authenticity over artifice. When you work with No Compromise Films, you get the real story — beautifully told, ethically captured.
